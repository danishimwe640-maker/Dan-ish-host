About Us

Igisubizo Academy helps nursery and primary students improve their academic performance.

Our Mission

To provide quality coaching and support that builds academic skills and confidence.

Our Vision

Every child equipped to succeed in school and life.

Since our founding, we have worked closely with parents, local schools, and volunteers to create learning pathways that are both practical and culturally relevant. We invest time in understanding individual learning gaps and build tailored lesson plans that strengthen reading, writing, and math skills while also supporting social development. Our long-term goal is to create a sustainable model that other communities can adapt — one that improves educational outcomes and opens opportunities for children across the region.
