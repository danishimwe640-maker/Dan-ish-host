Our Impact

We started with only 4 students and have grown steadily through partnerships with local communities and a commitment to individualized learning. Over the years we have refined our teaching methods to include small-group interventions, parent engagement sessions, and regular progress tracking; these approaches have helped students improve foundational literacy and numeracy skills, gain confidence in class participation, and achieve more consistent homework completion. Many families cite the academy's structured routines and mentoring as key reasons their children remain engaged and motivated to learn.

The broader impact extends beyond test scores: we have seen improvements in students' self-esteem, attendance, and social skills. Alumni of our programs are more likely to help peers with schoolwork, participate in community activities, and express aspirations for further education. Moving forward, we plan to scale our tutor training, expand learning resources, and deepen collaborations with local schools so that more children in Karongi can access consistent, high-quality academic support.
